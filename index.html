Game Rules:

Objective:

The goal of the game is to generate numbers and update your score based on specific conditions. Keep playing to increase your score, but be careful ‚Äì the game ends when your score drops to 0 or below!

How to Play:
  1. Starting the Game:
    • Click the "Play" button to start the game. Each click generates a new round with three random numbers between 1 and 9.
  2. Game Conditions:
    • Your score and game comment will be updated based on the following conditions:
      1. All Numbers Same:
        • If all three numbers are the same, you earn 1000 points.
        • Game Comment: "You were lucky this time, you got 1000 INR as all the numbers are the same".
      2. All Numbers Even or All Numbers Odd:
        • If all three numbers are either even or odd, you earn 300 points.
        • Game Comment: "You were lucky this time, you got 300 INR as all the numbers are even" or "You were lucky this time, you got 300 INR as all the numbers are odd".
      3. Numbers with a Difference of 1:
        • If the numbers have a difference of 1 between them (e.g., 1, 2, 3), you earn 800 points.
        • Game Comment: "You were lucky this time, you got 800 INR as all the numbers have a difference of 1".
      4. No Winning Conditions:
        • If none of the above conditions are met, you do not earn any points.
        • Game Comment: "You were not lucky this time".
  3. Updating Score:
    • Each round subtracts 100 points from your remaining score.
    • If your score drops to 0 or below, the game ends.
    • Game Comment: "Game Over".
  4. Restarting the Game:
    • If the game is over or you want to start fresh, click the "Restart" button to reset the game and your score.
    • You can restart the game at any time, even in the middle of a round.
  5. Game Over:
    • When your score is less than or equal to 0, the "Play" button will be disabled.
    • Click the "Restart" button to play again.
Tips:
  • Try to maximize your score by aiming for the conditions that give the highest points.
  • Monitor your score closely and plan your strategy to avoid the game ending too soon.
